About 9Ras Yoga

9Ras Yoga blends traditional yoga with modern wellness practices to help you reconnect with breath, balance and movement.

Your Yoga Journey

1. Arrive

Begin by arriving fully in the present moment. Slow your breath and settle your mind.

2. Breathe

Connect movement with breath. Let each inhale and exhale guide your flow.

3. Warm Up

Gentle stretches awaken the body and prepare your muscles for deeper movement.

4. Flow

Move through sequences that build strength, balance, and flexibility.

5. Balance

Explore poses that challenge your stability and deepen your focus.

6. Strength

Activate your core and develop strength through mindful posture.

7. Deep Stretch

Slow down and open the body with longer holds and deeper stretches.

8. Release

Allow tension to melt away and let your breath return to a calm rhythm.

9. Stillness

End in relaxation and meditation. Let the practice integrate fully.
